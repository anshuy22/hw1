About Us

Our Brand Culture

Our brand culture holds strong roots and game-changing visions. It stems from an array of influences and values:

  • Our vision to create a no-nonsense product and brand
  • Our dedication to researching and establishing sustainable practices and sharing this knowledge with our peers
  • Our relationship with our people, our suppliers and our tribe

Our People

We’re one small, happy family at Healthy Soapz, and we want to keep it that way. Our people’s wellbeing is very important to us,
so we always do what we can to make work feel less like, well, work. And as much as we love all the nice smells around
the office, we regularly swap them for some fresh air and a well-deserved day out.
